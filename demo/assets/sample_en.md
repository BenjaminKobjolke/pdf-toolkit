# Trip Notes — Lisbon

A quick packing and planning note, rendered from **Markdown**.

## Checklist

- Passport and *travel insurance*
- Camera, charger, spare battery
- Comfortable shoes for the hills

## Budget

Flights **240 €**, hotel **95 €** per night. Keep receipts for the
`expenses.md` summary.

> Tip: the tram 28 route covers most of the old town.
